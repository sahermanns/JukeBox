About Personalized Jukebox & Co.

Personiziled Jukebox & Co. wants to build a website to advertise a service they sell, this service is a personalized jukebox. Anyone who visits the site is able to try the free demo jukebox to encourage them to buy their own and customize it to their own tastes.

The demo jukebox on the main webpage prompts the user to choose an artist they like from the dropdown menu and then leads them to a list of song titles from the artist they selected. They can then select a specific track title to play. If the user doesn't recognize any of the artists in the list they can select a random song from the whole jukebox library to play instead.
